How to Play Dicey

  • Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold"
  • If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their current score and the player's turn continues.
  • If a player chooses to "hold", their current score is added to their global score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
